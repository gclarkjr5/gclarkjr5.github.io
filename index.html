Who is Gary?

An engineeringly-geeky-funhaving-social-soccerplaying-millenial who enjoys the subtle things.

Hi everyone! As you already know my name, I'll jump right into the stuff we're all more interested in. Born and bred on the East Coast (Harrisburg, PA), I've always enjoyed 3 things:

  1. Soccer.. I love sports, but soccer is my passion. I've played competitvely at multiple levels.
  2. Smiles... I really enjoy having social time to share laughs with close friends and loved ones. Preferably over some local craft brews I've never tried before.
  3. Asking why... Since I can remember, I've never stopped being curious to the world around me. Always learning about how things work, solving problems, and challenging the status quo.

I've never looked at myself as fitting into a particular category. I graduated with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering, but my job has attracted me to the Data Analytics & Computer Science world. I find it fun that I have an opportunity to mix them together and see what sort of insights come out of applying Big Data analytics on machinery and then visualizing them in a nice dashboard for attractive presentation.

Lastly, if I had all the free time in the world, I would constantly do these... Enjoy!
